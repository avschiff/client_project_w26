Networking & Connections

Build Meaningful Professional Relationships

Networking is a powerful tool for career development, opening doors to new opportunities, insights, and collaborations. Whether you're attending events, reaching out to professionals on LinkedIn, or engaging with alumni, effective networking can significantly enhance your career prospects.

On this page, you'll find strategies, tips, and resources to help you build and maintain a strong professional network that aligns with your career goals.

Networking Strategies

  • Attend Events: Participate in industry conferences, workshops, and UMSI networking events to meet professionals in your field.
  • Leverage Social Media: Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with alumni, join relevant groups, and share your professional journey.
  • Informational Interviews: Reach out to professionals for informational interviews to learn about their career paths and gain insights into the industry.
  • Follow Up: After meeting someone, send a personalized follow-up message to express your appreciation and keep the conversation going.
